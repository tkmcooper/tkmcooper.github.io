Who am I?

Welcome to the personal website of Terry Karl Michael Cooper!

I'm an IT Specialist based in Colchester with a huge passion for technology and learning new skills. I believe in the mantra that you should never stop learning and I am constantly keen to learn new skills that I can put to use in my professional and personal life.

Ever since I was a teenager I've had a great love for music. I'm always on the lookout for new bands/artists to listen to whether that's via browsing boxes of LPs in record shops, attending live local music gigs, listening to my favourite radio station or discovering playlists on Spotify. This combined with the above has led me to embark on my most ambitious project to date; developing an online music centre (called The Razor) as a place for me to catalogue and review albums, write feature articles and provide a discussion point for music fans worldwide. The site is currently set for launch in Autumn 2020.

Elsewhere I'm an eager student of History and I can often be found with my nose in a History book or the excellent BBC History magazine. I'm also a massive supporter of Chelsea FC and I love to unwind with a challenging crossword at either a local coffee-house or down the pub with a moreish pint of real ale!
